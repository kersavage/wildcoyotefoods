Ranch

Our signature product is the ranch dressing. We offer this in both regular and lite, and have added a few new ranch flavors as well. We have managed to create a homemade Ranch flavor and added 4 month shelf life. We always use real Buttermilk which helps keep the homemade Ranch flavor. Once people taste our Ranch they have a hard time eating any other. The Lite Ranch is our same great flavor with 63% less fat. It is hard to find a great Lite Ranch and we have it.

Salsa

We offer three salsas, Hot, Medium, and Mild. Each salsa is made with fresh ingredients and we like to make sure our customer is satisfied with their degree of hotness! For the mild we use various onions and Jalapeno Peppers, for the Medium we take that and add Serrano Peppers, and to make sure our Hot Salsa customers are happy we had Habaneros with the Serrano and Jalapeno Peppers. At any degree we know our customer is going to come back for more!

Thousand Island

We have a top of the line, great tasting Thousand Island that not only is great on salads but is even better on sandwiches and burgers. We have many customers who purchase our Thousand Island to use on their prepared sandwiches that they sell in their stores. We are proud to keep the love of Thousand Island continuing to the next generation.

Blue Cheese

The Blue Cheese dressing we use top of the line blue cheese crumbles, which causes the blue cheese flavor to stand out. We like to think that the chunks of blue cheese are what sets our dressing apart from any other Blue Cheese on the shelf. Thousand Island is making a come back amongst the younger customer.
